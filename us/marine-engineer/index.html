Marine Engineer — Your Expertise Keeps Vessels Running

Maintaining engines, managing systems at sea, diagnosing faults in challenging conditions — that skill is rare. Now you can get paid for it.

The Observer Programme pays experienced marine engineers to record their working day using lightweight smart glasses.

How it works

  • Wear smart glasses while you work
  • Record sessions from 30 minutes up
  • Get paid per recorded minute
  • All recordings are anonymised

What you'll earn

€55–€85 per recorded hour | €3,200–€4,500 per month (based on 20 recording days)

What we're looking for

  • 3+ years of marine engineering experience
  • Currently working
  • Hands-on engine/system work on vessels
  • Smartphone (iOS or Android)

All specialisms welcome: offshore vessel engineer, merchant navy engineer officer, small craft marine engineer, port maintenance engineer.
