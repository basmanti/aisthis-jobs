Delivery Driver — Your Route Knowledge Has Real Value

Loading efficiently, navigating under time pressure, handling parcels safely — your driving expertise has real value.

The Observer Programme pays experienced delivery drivers and couriers to record their working day using lightweight smart glasses and a wristband.

How it works

  • Wear smart glasses and a wristband during your route
  • Record sessions from 30 minutes up
  • Spend a few minutes after each session confirming what you did
  • Get paid per recorded minute
  • All recordings are anonymised — no faces, no voices, no identifying details

What you'll earn

€7–€10 per recorded hour

€28–€40 per recording day (based on ~4 hours)

€400–€600 per month (based on 20 recording days)

First Observers earn a launch bonus.

What we're looking for

  • 1+ years of delivery/courier experience
  • Currently working — employed, self-employed, or gig
  • Active driving and delivery work: loading, route driving, last-mile delivery
  • Valid driving licence for your vehicle class
  • Smartphone (iOS or Android)

All specialisms welcome: last-mile parcel delivery, grocery/food delivery driver, HGV driver, refrigerated delivery driver, same-day courier.

We provide everything

  • Smart glasses — shipped to you, pre-configured
  • Full onboarding (~30 minutes)
  • Dedicated contact person

About the Observer Programme

The Observer Programme by Aisthis works with professionals across 100+ trades to document how skilled work actually happens.

Applications are reviewed with the help of AI. We'll be in touch if there's a match.

Quick apply — 3 questions

  1. What type of deliveries do you make?
  2. How many years of driving/delivery experience do you have?
  3. Are you currently working as a driver?

No extra hours. No second job. Just your normal route — with extra income.
