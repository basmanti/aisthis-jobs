Bricklayer — Every Course You Lay Has Real Value

Consistent gauge, clean joints, plumb corners — that's skill you can't learn from a video. Now you can get paid for it.

🇪🇺 Bricklayers and masons are on the European Commission's official list of critical shortage professions. Your skills are in high demand. Record them. Get paid.

The Observer Programme pays experienced bricklayers to record their working day using lightweight smart glasses and a wristband. You do your normal work — we capture the expertise.

How it works

  • Wear smart glasses (lighter than safety glasses) and a wristband while you work
  • Record sessions from 30 minutes up — fits around your day
  • Spend a few minutes after each session confirming what tasks you did in the app
  • Get paid per recorded minute. The more you record, the more you earn
  • All recordings are anonymised — no faces, no voices, no identifying details

What you'll earn

You're paid per minute of accepted footage — on top of your regular income.

€14–€21 per recorded hour

€56–€84 per recording day (based on ~4 hours)

€1,000–€1,400 per month (based on 20 recording days)

First Observers in a specialism or region earn a launch bonus. Extra earnings possible through referral bonuses and specialist recording tasks.

What we're looking for

  • 3+ years of experience as a bricklayer or mason
  • Currently working — employed, self-employed, or freelance
  • Hands-on work: bricklaying, blockwork, pointing, repointing, stonework
  • Smartphone (iOS or Android)

All specialisms welcome: structural bricklayer, repointing and restoration, dry stone waller, block paver, retaining wall builder.

We provide everything

  • Smart glasses — shipped to you, pre-configured
  • Full onboarding and support (~30 minutes)
  • Dedicated contact person
  • Biometric wristband if you don't own a compatible fitness tracker

About the Observer Programme

The Observer Programme by Aisthis works with professionals across 100+ trades to document how skilled work actually happens. Bricklaying — the rhythm, the consistency, the eye for level — is craft knowledge worth recording.

Applications are reviewed with the help of AI. We'll be in touch if there's a match.
