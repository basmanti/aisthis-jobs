Automotive Assembly Worker — Precision on the Line

Fitting components, hitting cycle times, maintaining quality at speed — your production skill has real value.

The Observer Programme pays automotive assembly workers to record their shifts using lightweight smart glasses and a wristband.

How it works

  • Wear smart glasses and a wristband during your shift
  • Record sessions from 30 minutes up
  • Spend a few minutes after each session confirming what tasks you did
  • Get paid per recorded minute
  • All recordings are anonymised

What you'll earn

€15–€18 per recorded hour

€60–€72 per recording day (based on ~4 hours)

€900–€1,100 per month (based on 20 recording days)

First Observers earn a launch bonus.

What we're looking for

  • 1+ years of assembly line experience
  • Currently working in an automotive plant
  • Hands-on assembly work: fitting, fastening, testing, quality checking
  • Smartphone (iOS or Android)

All roles welcome: body shop assembly, trim and final assembly, powertrain assembly, quality control inspector, paint shop operative.

We provide everything

  • Smart glasses — shipped to you, pre-configured
  • Full onboarding (~30 minutes)
  • Dedicated contact person

About the Observer Programme

The Observer Programme by Aisthis works with professionals across 100+ trades to document how skilled work actually happens.

Applications are reviewed with the help of AI. We'll be in touch if there's a match.

Quick apply — 3 questions

  1. What assembly area do you work in?
  2. How long have you worked in automotive assembly?
  3. Are you currently working?

No extra hours. Just your normal shift — with extra income.
